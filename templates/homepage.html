Welcome to Alumni Connexa

We are proud to connect students and alumni of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University (Diploma Wing). Stay connected, explore opportunities, and celebrate success with our alumni network!

Uniting Alumni, Enabling Opportunities Inspiring Success, and Building a Legacy

Alumni Connexa, the official alumni platform of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University, fosters strong connections and career growth. It offers networking, job portals, mentorship, and success story tracking to empower alumni. A secure donation portal and event management system encourage engagement and contributions. The platform bridges past and present, creating a thriving alumni ecosystem. Alumni Connexa enables alumni to connect, contribute, and make a lasting impact.
